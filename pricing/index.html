Frequently Asked Questions

  • If I don't have a Credit Card, how can I top-up my SMSGlobal account?

    We can provide you with an Invoice which you can pay by EFT – call our sales team.

  • What methods of payment do you provide?

    We offer different payment options Credit Card, PayPal, EFT. Auto Top up and low balance reminders are available.

  • Do you offer a Free Trial?

    Yes we certainly offer a free trial please call or Form Submission. Sandbox testing is also available please contact support@smsglobal.com.

  • Do you offer wholesale rates/Custom plans?

    Yes we provide custom packages based on your requirements please get in touch with our friendly Sales Team.

  • Do you offer discount to Not for profit organisations and Schools?

    Yes, we offer special discounts to NFP and Schools.

  • Can you contact me when my balance is running low?

    A low balance reminder can be set up in your account and you can opt to receive an email and SMS when credits hits a certain threshold.
